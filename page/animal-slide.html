Mammals

Mammals are animals that breathes air.They have backbone and grow hair at some point during its life.As we have know , all female mammal produce milk to feed their babies. There are between 4,500 and 5,000 species of mammals in the world.Mammals are the most Intelligent of all living creatures include chimpanzees , dolphins , human and more .Mammals include a wide variety of animals , from mouse to human , blue whale.They have got warm blood and their body remains about the same all the time , even though the temperature of their surroundings may change.

Bird

Birds are the only animal that can fly exceptionally Penguin ,Steamer duck ,Weka ,Ostrich ,Kiwi, Kakapo ,Takahe ,Cassowary can't fly. There are total between 9,000 to 11,000 species of birds in the world today. Birds reproduce by laying eggs with hard shells that hatch externally.
