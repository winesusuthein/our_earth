Tiger

Tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus panthera.It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside. A Tiger can grow to around 10 feet long and weigh over 400 pounds. Today tigers live in Asia continent including countries such as India, Burma, Russia, China, Laos, Thailand, and Indonesia.

Monkeys

Monkeys are mammals and they are the most similar animal with human. Monkey have general, nearly 200 species and Chimpanzee is most intelligent species in all. Human DNA is around 98% similar to chimpanzee.Most monkeys live in tropical rainforest of Asia , Africa and Central and South America or the savannas of Africa. They have to stay away from dangerous animals like big snake, crocodiles, and leopards.

Deer

Deer are wild animal that belongs to live in Cervidae family. There are over 60 Different species of deer worldwide. Deer can find on all continents except Antarctica. They live on mountains areas to warm and wet rainforests.Male deer are called buck , female deer are called doe or hind and young deer are called fawn.
