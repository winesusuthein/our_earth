Octopus

Octopus are one of the interesting sea animal. They have bulbous heads, large eyes and eight useful arms. They also have three special heart which is partly a consequence of having blue blood, another two hearts are pump blood through the gills, where it picks up oxygen.A central heart then circulates the oxygenated blood to the rest of the body to provide energy for organs and muscles.

Goldfish

The Goldfish is a freshwater fish in the family Cyprinidae of order Cypriniformes. It is commonly kept as a pet in indoor aquariums, and is one of the most popular aquarium fish. Goldfish released into the wild have become an invasive pest in parts of North America. Goldfish can alive for 10-15 years. Goldfish can remember memory until at least three months. Goldfish have two sets of paired fins and three sets of single fins.

Salmon

Salmons include seven species of Pacific salmon and one species of Atlantic salmon. They're found in tributaries of the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans, and most species are anadromous: They are born in streams and rivers. They migrate out to the open sea, and then return to freshwater again to reproduce.
