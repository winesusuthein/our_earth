Bee

Bees are winged insects which have two wings on their back.They are known for their roles in pollination. Western honey bee , is known as producing honey. Bees are a monophyletic lineage within the family Apoidea. There is special habitat for bees which is on their back called Sting.

Ant

Ants are the most annoying animals in the world. You can find them everywhere include food , dirty place , drinks and more.They are too tiny. They have six legs and they can carry food with their mouth. There are between 13,800 and 22,000 species of ant. Ants are social insects , which mean they live in group or colony.Colonies live inside of the nests that can built in trees, underground.Ant colonies are highly organized, usually ruled by a single queen, and each ant has specific jobs to do.

Ladybug

Ladybug are insects with red wing and black dots but there are other ladybug without dots and different colors. When a ladybug fly , they beat their wings about 85 times per second. There are about 5,000 different species of ladybugs in the world.A female ladybug can lay as many as 1,000 egg at one time.
