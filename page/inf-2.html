Peacock

The word “peacock” does not actually apply to both male and female birds. It’s only the males that are called peacocks, while the females are called peahens. Together, the collective name for them is “peafowl.There are just three different types of species in the peafowl family. Two of these originate in Asia, and the other is of African descent.Their lifespan are up to 15 years. peacock makes a loud and nasal call “may-awe” mostly at dawn and dusk.

Robin

Robins are popular birds for their warm orange breast, cheery song, and early appearance at the end of winter.Robin have about 2,900 feathers.Only the male robin sings the "true robin song".There are only around 50 species of robin bird in average.

Parrot

Parrot are know as their colorful feathers.Parrots can range in size from about 3.5 to 40 inches (8.7 to 100 centimeters).Weigh is about  2.25 to 56 ounces (64 g to 1.6 kg), on average.Most wild parrots live in the warm areas like Mexico, Australia, South America and Central America.Most parrots are social birds that live in groups called flocks.
